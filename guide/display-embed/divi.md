# Divi 5 Module (Pro)

FluentPlayer Pro includes native **Divi 5 modules** for both the video player and playlist. Add them to any Divi row or section and configure every setting directly inside the Divi builder panel — no shortcode required.

::: info Pro feature
The Divi 5 module requires **FluentPlayer Pro**. The free plugin supports [Shortcode](/shortcode) and [Gutenberg Block](/block) embeds.
:::

::: warning Divi 5 only
These modules are built for **Divi 5**. They are not available in Divi 4 or the Divi Builder plugin for classic themes. If you are on Divi 4, use the [Shortcode](/shortcode) embed instead.
:::

## Adding the FluentPlayer Module

1. Open a page in the **Divi** visual builder.
2. Click the blue **+** button inside any row or section to insert a new module.

![Divi Builder](/guide/public/display-embed/divi/divi-1.webp)

3. In the **Insert Module Or Row** popup, type **fluent player** in the search bar.
4. Click the **Fluent Player** module to insert it.

![Fluent Player Module](/guide/public/display-embed/divi/fluent-player-2.webp)

5. In the right panel, open the **Content** tab. Under the **Fluent Player** section, click the **Select Media** dropdown and choose your media item. The player renders live in the Divi canvas.
6. The **General Settings** panel appears below with options including **Title**, **Title Overlay**, **Aspect Ratio**, and **Load Strategy**.
7. Click **Save** (top right) to apply your changes.

![Choose Media](/guide/public/display-embed/divi/choose-media-3.webp)

## Adding the FluentPlayer Playlist Module

1. Click the blue **+** button to insert a new module and type **fluent player** in the search bar.
2. Click the **Fluent Player Playlist** module to insert it.

![Fluent Player Playlist Module](/guide/public/display-embed/divi/playlist-module-4.webp)

3. In the **Content** tab, under the **Fluent Player Playlist** section, click the **Select Playlist** dropdown and choose your playlist. The playlist renders live in the Divi canvas.
4. The **General Settings** panel appears below with options including **Playlist Title**, **Autoplay**, **Continuous Play**, **Show Previous / Next Buttons**, and **Loop Playlist**.
5. Click **Save** (top right) to apply your changes.

![Choose Playlist](/guide/public/display-embed/divi/choose-playlist-5.webp)

## Available Settings

The Divi module exposes the same settings as the [Gutenberg Block](/block-editor):

| Panel | What you can configure |
|-------|----------------------|
| **General Settings** | Title, title overlay, thumbnail, aspect ratio, load strategy |
| **Branding & Appearance** | Logo, custom colors, player skin overrides |
| **Interactive Layers** | Email capture, CTA, Hotspots, Fluent Forms, Ads |
| **Chapters** | Chapter markers and titles |
| **Subtitles** | Subtitle tracks and caption styling |
| **Multiple Languages** | Audio language tracks |
| **Timed Content (Pro)** | Content blocks that appear below the player at exact timestamps |
| **Overlays (Pro)** | Text and button overlays |
| **Advanced** | Custom CSS, visibility rules |
