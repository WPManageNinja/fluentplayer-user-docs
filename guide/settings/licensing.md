# Licensing (Pro)

Your **FluentPlayer Pro license** unlocks the Pro features and enables automatic plugin updates. After installing FluentPlayer Pro, activate your license once so the plugin can verify your subscription and deliver updates.

::: info Pro feature
Licensing applies to **FluentPlayer Pro**. The free plugin does not require a license. Remember that Pro also needs the free FluentPlayer plugin installed and active.
:::

## Activating your license

1. In your WordPress admin, go to **FluentPlayer → Settings → Licensing**.
2. Enter your **license key** (from your account or purchase receipt).
3. Click **Activate**.

Once activated, the status shows your license as active, Pro features are unlocked, and update notifications will appear when a new version is released.

## Refreshing your license

If your plan changed or the status looks out of date, click **Refresh** to re-check your license with the licensing server without re-entering the key.

## Deactivating your license

Click **Deactivate** to release the license on this site — for example, before moving the site to a new domain or freeing up an activation for another site. Deactivating disables Pro updates until you activate again.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | What to check |
|---------|---------------|
| Activation fails | Confirm the key is copied in full and matches your active subscription. |
| Pro features missing after activation | Make sure both the free **FluentPlayer** and **FluentPlayer Pro** plugins are active. |
| No update notifications | Ensure the license is **active**; click **Refresh** to re-sync. |
