# Licensing

Your **license key** connects Fluent Player Pro to your account so you can unlock Pro features and receive automatic plugin updates. You manage it from the **License** screen in the Fluent Player settings.

::: tip
A license key applies to **Fluent Player Pro**. The free plugin works without a key — you only need one once you install Pro.
:::

## Where to Find It

Go to **Fluent Player → Settings → License**. The screen shows your current license status and a field to enter or update your key.

## Activating Your License

1. Copy your license key from your account / purchase receipt.
2. Paste it into the **License Key** field.
3. Click **Activate**.

Once activated, the status badge changes to **Active**, and your key is shown in a masked form (for example `ABCD••••WXYZ`) so it isn't fully exposed on screen.

## License Status

The screen shows a status badge that reflects the current state of your license, such as:

- **Active** — the license is valid; Pro features and updates are enabled.
- Other states (for example expired or invalid) appear with a readable label so you know what action to take.

If you've just renewed or changed something on your account, use the **refresh** action to re-check the latest status from the server.

## Deactivating Your License

If you're moving the license to another site, click **Deactivate**. This frees the activation so you can use the key elsewhere. Deactivating disables Pro updates on the current site until a key is activated again.

::: warning
Keep your license active on production sites to continue receiving updates and security fixes for Fluent Player Pro.
:::
